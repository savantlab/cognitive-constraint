## Crowdfunding (Non-Traditional)

### **Academic Crowdfunding Platforms**

**Experiment.com** (for research projects)
- Goal: $25-75K
- Donors: Other researchers, institutions
- **Fit:** Moderate - better for specific research than platform

**Kickstarter / Indiegogo**
- Goal: $50-150K
- **Pitch:** "First journal that pays researchers"
- Rewards: Early access, naming rights, acknowledgments
- **Fit:** Risky - might look unserious for academic venture

**Patreon / Substack**
- Monthly subscription model
- Academic supporters pay $10-100/month
- Goal: $2-10K/month (supplemental, not primary)
- **Fit:** Good for community building, not serious funding
